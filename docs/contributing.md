## Community
If you would like to contribute to StealthChanger, all of our projects are hosted on [Github](https://github.com/DraftShift){target="_blank"}. [Pull requests](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/creating-a-pull-request){target="_blank"} are welcome and encouraged. 

### Updating the Documentation

- Submit a descriptive [pull request](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/creating-a-pull-request){target="_blank"} of the feature/issue you are addressing.
- Keep the documented information concise.

### Submitting Usermods

- Create a directory in UserMods of your github username.
- Ensure the mod is in it's own folder, do not put everything in the root of your UserMod folder.
- Include a clear README.md per usermod, you may have a general one as well but each usermod requires it's own README.md.
- Ensure the README.md contains all credits with links, respect and follows GPL.
- Submit a descriptive [pull request](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/creating-a-pull-request){target="_blank"}
- Be available to support your usermod. It's expected that you will be able to answer any questions that may arise surrounding your usermod.
